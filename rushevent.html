Rush Event Schedule

Basic Info

Rush Events always start on Monday or Tuesday of the week, depending on Major or Minor Rush, respectively.

‚åõRush Order Sequence‚è≥

Major Rush | Minor Rush
The | denotes major rush on left side and starts on Monday, and minor rush on right side and starts on Tuesday.

Week 1 - Prayer Rush and Family Prayer Rush
Week 2 - Tech Rush | Mount Rush
Week 3 - Soul Rush | Pal Rush
Week 4 - Relic Rush | Skill Rush
Week 5 - Prayer Rush and Family Prayer Rush

Prayer Rush is the only week where there are no rush starts on Tuesday
Currently, this cycles monthly and so we should always save our resources once a rush event ends for next time.

  • Prayer Rush - 25.5k fruits per round, 102k total | Rewards: Relic Shards
  • Tech Rush - 120k ores per round, 480k total | Rewards: Divine Hammers
  • Mount Rush - 800 winders per round, 3.2k total | Rewards: Divine Hammers
  • Soul Rush - 5k essences per round, 20k total | Rewards: Divine Hammers
  • Pal Rush - 2k pulls per round, 8k pulls total | Rewards: Relic Shards
    (About 1713 tix/round, about 6855 tix total)
  • Relic Rush - 225k shards per round, 900k total | Rewards: Divine Hammers
  • Skill Rush - 2k pulls per round, 8k pulls total | Rewards: Relic Shards
    (About 1713 tix/round, about 6855 tix total)


    • üéÅRush TipsüéÅ

    Prayer Rush
    Buy premium fertilizers daily to speed up premium seeds on demand
    Once you have at least 30 premium seeds, buy the Bountiful Harvest from Parking War to get 50% extra fruits for 30 harvests.

    Tech Rush
    Save the Speedup Tickets to use during this event.

    Mount Rush
    Perfect event to dump your gems. 35k gems = 1000 winders

    Pal/Skill Rush
    Not exactly a tip but note that 30 tickets = 35 pulls, so your actual pull for ranking is tickets x 1.167 (i.e. 2000 tickets = 2334 pulls)

    Relic Rush
    Be sure to level ALL of your Relics equally--yes, even the ones you don't use. They are Overall Stats, meaning passive stats.
    I recommend leveling them all in incremental of 5 levels at a time.
